# MESH Health Study Guide

## Self Esteem and Body Image

### self-image
mental picture of one's abilities, appearance, and personality based on experiences and interactions with others.

### self-esteem
confidence in one's own worth and abilities.

### body-image
subjective mental image of one's own body; established based on self-observation and the reactions of others.

#### Influences on body image (4)

- culture and society
- family
- friends and peers
- the media

### stereotype
a widely held, oversimplified image or idea of a particular type of person or thing.


## Eating Disorders

**body dysmorphic disorder**: a mental health disorder in which someone can not stop thinking about perceived defects or flaws regarding appearance.

### Anorexia Nervosa

An intense fear of weight gain, resulting in severely cutting down caloric intake. It causes extreme weight loss.

### Bulimia

A type of binge eating disorder

Binge eating followed by purging through vomiting or laxatives (constipation treatments).

### Binge eating disorder
recurrent episodes of eating unusually large amounts of food.

### Treatment options

- nutrition counseling
- medical care
- talk therapy

## Teens for Life

### Warning signs of suicide

- previous attempt
- verbal threats
- changes in behavior
- signs of depression
- problems in school
- themes of death
- substance abuse
- giving away possessions

### Actions to take if worried about a friend (of suicide)

- talk to a trusted adult
- start a conversation
- ask direct questions
- act: safety over secrets

## Counseler Presentation

### Warning signs of depression

There are almost always warning signs of suicide.

- loss of interest in activities once enjoyed
- weight loss/gain
- too little or too much sleeping
- loss of energy or increased fatigue
- feeling worthless or guilty
- difficulty thinking, concentrating, or making decisions
- having feelings of self harm
