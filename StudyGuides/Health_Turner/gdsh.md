## Tri-Valley Haven Healthy Relationships

### Healthy Relationships
#### Qualities
- fairness and equity
- acceptance
- safety
- works through problems
- respectful disagreements
- enjoys being together
- encourages growth
- healthy boundaries
- open minded

#### Before Getting into a Relationship
- know yourself
- build emotional maturity
- set boundaries early
- make sure you're ready

#### Types of Communication
- **aggressive**: To control or win, to appear strong.
- **passive**: To keep the peace and remain hidden.
- **assertive**: To communicate accurately and effectively.

#### Personal Boundaries
- physical: Personal space, toch, privacy
- emotional:
- sexual: consent, comfort, communication
- digital: consent, connections, privacy

**F**reely given

**S**pecific

#### How to Stop Victim Blaming
- believe survivors
- offer resources
- avoid judging
- the only person responsible is the abuser

#### The Cycle of Violence
honeymoon &rarr; tension building &rarr; explosive

#### Bystander Intervention
- direct
- distract
- delegate (document)

#### Conflict Resolution &#9733;
- skip the silent treatment
- use "I" statements

- perspectives
